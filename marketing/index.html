Medicare Billing Codes

Healthcare partners bill Medicare directly using Safe4Seniors documentation

Code Name Rate Requirements
CCM Chronic Care Management ~$62/mo 2+ chronic conditions, 20+ min/month
RPM Remote Patient Monitoring ~$120/mo 16+ days data collection
PCM Principal Care Management ~$62-83/mo ONE serious condition, 30+ min/month
TCM Transitional Care Management ~$168-239 Contact within 2 days of discharge

Example: A hospital with 100 CCM patients generates $74,400/year in Medicare reimbursement using our documentation, while paying us $54,000/year. Net partner gain: $20,400/year + reduced penalties.
